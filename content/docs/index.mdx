## Welcome to Tandaan

Tandaan is a collaborative note workspace with a **replay layer** for product storytelling. Instead of just tracking *what* changed, Tandaan captures *why* decisions were made and *how* ideas evolved, then packages that history into shareable, chapterized replays.

### Start here

- [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started) — Install Tandaan, create your first note, and generate a replay link in under five minutes.
- [Replay API Overview](/docs/replay-api) — Learn how share-link generation works and explore the replay payload schema.
- [Timeline Query Concepts](/docs/timeline-query) — Query your editing history with natural language and jump to the exact moment a decision was made.

### What makes Tandaan different

1. **Realtime collaboration is native.** Every keystroke syncs across participants through Liveblocks and Yjs, with presence indicators showing who is writing where.
2. **Replays are chapterized for async review.** Long editing sessions are segmented into meaningful chapters so reviewers can skip to decision moments instead of watching everything.
3. **Decision context is preserved and queryable.** Every chapter can carry rationale tags, source references, and owner notes that remain attached to the revision forever.
